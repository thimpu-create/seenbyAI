What does {{ product_name }} actually check? +

Five areas: technical health (HTTPS, sitemap, robots, canonical tags, response time), structured data (Organization, WebSite, FAQPage, Article, HowTo schema), trust signals (about, contact, author bios, visible dates), content structure (direct answers, heading hierarchy, depth), and a live AI citation test against configured search providers.

How is the AI score calculated? +

Each of the five categories is scored independently, then combined into a single 0–100 AI score with a letter grade. The report breaks down exactly which checks passed, failed, or are missing in each category.
