About

Search is changing. We help you keep up with it.

{{ product_name }} started from a simple observation: more people are finding businesses through AI-generated answers than through a list of blue links. An AI Overview, a Perplexity answer, or a ChatGPT search result either cites your site or it doesn't — and most websites have no way of knowing which.

What we believe

A website can be well-designed and still be invisible to an AI crawler. Good SEO and good AI-search readiness overlap, but they aren't the same thing — an answer engine needs to extract a clear claim, trust where it came from, and confirm the page is structured well enough to quote. We built {{ product_name }} to measure that gap directly, rather than guessing at it.

What the audit actually does

Every scan checks five things: technical crawlability, structured data, trust signals, content structure, and a live citation test against real search providers. The report doesn't stop at a score — it lists what's missing and what to fix first, in the order that matters most.

Who it's for

Marketing teams, agencies, and site owners who want to know whether their content is actually reaching readers through the surfaces that increasingly stand between them and a search box.
